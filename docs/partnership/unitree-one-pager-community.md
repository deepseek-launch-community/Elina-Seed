## The Opportunity

Unitree builds the world's most advanced humanoid robots — H1, G1, Go2, B2. They move with grace, work in extreme environments, and are already deployed across China's factories, homes, and research labs.

But there's one thing missing: **memory.**

Today, every interaction with a Unitree robot starts from zero. It doesn't remember the user's name, their preferences, their history. It's a tool — not a companion.

Elina OS changes that.

---

## What Elina OS Offers

Elina OS is an on-device **memory + safety + identity** layer built for robots. It runs entirely locally, with no cloud dependency, and integrates seamlessly with Unitree's existing ROS 2 stack.

| Layer | Function | Why Unitree Cares |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **Hardware-Rooted Identity** | ML-DSA-87 keys in Secure Enclave | Enables per-robot algorithmic filing; meets HEIS 2026 compliance |
| **Persistent Memory** | HMAC-signed SQLite, AES-256-GCM | Robots remember users across sessions — builds trust, enables care |
| **Constitutional Safety** | Trauma lexicon + valence thresholds | Prevents harmful responses; reduces liability |
| **Ministry Filing Layer** | Pre-formatted for MIIT/MCA/MEE grants | Turns compliance into revenue |
